My top 3 movies of all time

Dune

Cool sci-fi movie based on Frank Herbert novel Dune and its five sequels.
Rating: 10/10

La La Land

An amazing musical romantic comedy-drama with Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone.
Rating: 12/10

Gladiator

A former Roman General sets out to exact vengeance against the corrupt emperor who murdered his family and sent him into slavery.
Rating: 10/10
